WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Abeer Fatima Foodstuff Trading

At Abeer Fatima Foodstuff, we specialize in the wholesale distribution of premium chocolates, beverages, and confectionery products. We proudly supply supermarkets, grocery stores, convenience stores, wholesalers, and retail businesses with trusted brands at competitive wholesale prices.

With a strong distribution network, reliable inventory, and fast delivery, we help businesses keep their shelves stocked with the products customers love. Operating from Deira, Dubai, we leverage optimized global logistics to connect premium brands with retailers and trade partners across international markets, securing maximum ROI for your business.

Genuine Quality Sourcing We verify every batch, ensuring correct labels, proper shelf-life, and authentic manufacturing origins.
Full Container Load (FCL) Logistics Experts in consolidated containers, custom clearances, and multi-modal freight transport.
